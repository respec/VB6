General Description

HEM-1d is a one-dimensional intra-tidal model, suitable for application to sectionally homogeneous tidal rivers and estuaries.

 

 

Model methods include the following:

Finite difference method.

 

Limitations

Limited to tidal river and estuaries without substantial lateral variation and vertical stratification.
