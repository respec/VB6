General Description:

ISIS is a comprehensive suite of software tools for analyzing the behavior of rivers, canals, flood plains and estuaries. ISIS provides the capability to model hydraulics, water quality, hydrology and sediment transport in both simple and complex open channel systems. ISIS assists engineers and managers in developing cost-effective designs and strategies in river engineering, catchment planning and operational management.

ISIS Quality is a fully featured water quality simulator, and includes advection / diffusion of conservative and decaying pollutants, water temperature, sediment transport, interaction of quality determinands with sediments, phytoplankton and pH. The water quality engine can be used in conjunction with a full hydrodynamic simulation to simulate water quality processes in detail, or can be driven by simpler flow routing methods for less detailed applications. The user is able to specify the processes included in the simulation, providing focus on the specific needs of the particular study and improved productivity

This is a comprehensive MS Windows - based package of river, estuary, canal and catchment modeling tools with a variety of modules covering hydrodynamic and steady state hydraulics, water-quality processes, sediment transport, flood hydrology, a deterministic catchment model and sophisticated SCADA system modeling. Applications range from canal design, sedimentation and operational control on macro-irrigation schemes in Pakistan, to catchment management looking at flooding problems on the Rios Parana, Paraguay and Uruguay (in South America), to water-resources engineers looking at losses from reservoir releases on the largest river in South Africa (the Orange River).

Model Use Features

  • fast data input, checking and editing

  • comprehensive on-line help

  • powerful graphics including network visualization and animation, and report quality printed output

  • graphic and text export to GIS, CAD, word-processing and spreadsheet software

  • schematic or map views of the modeled system are available, through which model data and results can be accessed

  • sections, longitudinal profiles and time series are all easily displayed, with observed data superimposed where available.

  • the map view provides simple flood extent mapping and animation, and this can be displayed on a map background.

  • graphical outputs can be displayed on-screen in a report quality format and then either printed or output to DXF for import into a CAD system.

  • a wide range of tabular outputs are available, and the software also includes a number of statistical functions for further output analysis

Model Support

Users Manual:

All ISIS modules are supplied with comprehensive user documentation. Work book titled 'Teaching Hydraulics with ISIS', is also available for academic users.

The ISIS help system provides on-line help throughout the software, and this is supported by detailed tutorials and extensive reference manuals. The manuals are supplied both as printed documentation and in digital format suitable for on-screen use.
